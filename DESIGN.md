# Design System

## Register

Product UI. Quiet, operational, dense enough for real work.

## Scene

A founder or marketing lead reviews ad performance on a laptop during the workday, needs fast decisions, and does not want platform jargon.

## Color Strategy

Restrained product palette with a small semantic accent system.

- Canvas: warm gray tinted neutral
- Surface: off-white
- Text: deep ink
- Primary: teal for trusted action
- Warning: amber for risk
- Critical: red for wasted spend
- Google: blue accent
- Meta: indigo accent

## Typography

Use the system UI stack. Fixed type scale. No display fonts. Labels should be compact and readable.

## Components

- Side navigation for product modules
- Dense panels for operational surfaces
- Status pills for confidence, platform, and risk
- Tables for campaign architecture
- Inline approval controls
- Print-friendly campaign book export

## Interaction

Primary flows should be progressive:

1. Ask intake questions.
2. Generate architecture.
3. Review logic, forecast, and risks.
4. Save final plan.
5. Export campaign book.
6. Build inside native Google Ads and Meta Ads dashboards.

## Content Rules

Use plain business language first, technical terms second. Explain why a campaign structure exists. Label all estimates with source and confidence.
